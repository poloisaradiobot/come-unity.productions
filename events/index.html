About the night

Elevate Saturdays

Edmonton's bi-weekly house and deep house night — every Saturday July & August at The Cask & Barrel. Curated by residents Poloismz and Sotero.

The room runs warm and deep: vocal-driven house, rolling grooves, occasional Afro house swing. Sets are built live, reading the crowd. No formula. Low lights. Good people playing good music.

The dancefloor is the point.
