about the night

elevate saturdays

Edmonton's bi-weekly house and deep house night — every other Saturday at The Cask & Barrel. Curated by come-unity, resident DJ PoloIsMZ, and resident Sotero.

The room runs warm and deep: vocal-driven house, rolling grooves, occasional Afro house swing. Sets are built live, reading the crowd. No formula. Low lights. Good people.

the dancefloor is the point.
