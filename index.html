about

Our purpose

Elevate Saturdays is Edmonton's home for house and deep house — a bi-weekly night built on the idea that the dancefloor is a place people actually find each other.

Every other Saturday at The Cask & Barrel. Low lights, deep low end, a crowd that came to move.

House music. Community. Edmonton's underground.

The next step

What started as a residency became a standing invitation — Elevate Saturdays, every two weeks, an open floor for the city's house heads and the curious alike.

COME-UNITY exists to grow that further: more nights, more artists, more of Edmonton's electronic music community in one room.
