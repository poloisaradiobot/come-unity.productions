OUR PURPOSE

COME-UNITY is an Edmonton-based music collective and events brand dedicated to growing the city's house and electronic music community from the ground up.

Founded by Poloismz, come-unity exists to create spaces where dancers, DJs, producers, and listeners feel genuinely welcomed — not just entertained. Every Elevate Saturday at The Cask & Barrel is an extension of that mission: a bi-weekly house and deep house night built around feel, not formula. Low lights, deep low end, a crowd that came to move.

House music. Community. Edmonton's underground..

THE NEXT STEP

What started as a residency became a standing invitation — Elevate Saturdays, every two weeks, an open floor for the city's house heads and the curious alike. The sets move from warm, vocal-driven house through to deeper, rolling grooves — built track by track, reading the room as it fills.

COME-UNITY exists to grow that further: more nights, more artists, more of Edmonton's electronic music community in one or more rooms — building something rooted in this city rather than borrowed from anywhere else.
