poloismz

Edmonton, AB · est. 2026
House Deep House Tech House Electropop-Synthpop Alternative Dance

Poloismz is the DJ and founder behind come-unity — Edmonton's bi-weekly house night, Elevate Saturdays. Working across house, deep house, and the textures between, the sets are built around feel rather than formula: reading the room, building slow, going deep.

Bilingual, rooted in Edmonton, and shaped by a background that runs from music education to community building — every Elevate Saturday is a direct extension of that; a space that feels like it belongs to the people in it, not just the person behind the decks.

the floor is yours. i'm just the one who got here first.

in the mix

Sets typically move from warm, vocal-driven deep house through to rolling, minimal-leaning grooves — tempo staying tight around 110–128 BPM. Chicago soul influence on one end, tech house swing on the other. Never pre-planned. Always live, always reactive.
